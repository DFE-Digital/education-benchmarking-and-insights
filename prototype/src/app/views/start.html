{{ serviceName }}

This service helps schools view and improve their spending. It is replacing View my financial insights service and Schools Financial Benchmarking.

Anyone can use it to compare schools, academies or trusts across England.

At a school level

You can:

  • see how spending compares with similar schools
  • enter your own data to see how spending less or more affects benchmarking position
  • plan and forecast future scenarios

At a trust or local authority level

You can:

  • get an overview of all your schools and see how spending compares with similar schools
  • identify where schools need more support with their spending
  • see how your spending compares with similar trusts
  • view risks based on the budget forecast return (trusts only)

You can also download data and findings to use in reports.

You may need to sign in to your Department for Education account to access some parts of the service.
